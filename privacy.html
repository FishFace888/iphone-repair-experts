Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your personal information

Last updated: May 2026

Who we are

iPhone Repair Experts is a mobile iPhone repair service operating across Kent. We are the data controller responsible for your personal information. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us on 07770 971253.

What information we collect

When you contact us for a quote or to book a repair, we collect the following information:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your email address
  • Your postcode
  • Your iPhone model and the issue you are experiencing
  • Your marketing preference — whether you would like to receive communications from us

How we collect your information

We collect information when you contact us via our website contact form, WhatsApp, phone, email or any other messaging service. All information is collected directly from you.

How we use your information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and provide a quote
  • Book and carry out your repair
  • Contact you regarding your repair or follow up after the service is complete
  • Send you marketing communications if you have opted in to receive them

Marketing communications

We will only send you marketing communications if you have explicitly opted in to receive them. These may be sent via email, SMS or WhatsApp. You can unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time by contacting us on 07770 971253 and we will action your request immediately.

How we store your information

Your information may be stored in our customer management systems, email platforms and messaging services. We take reasonable steps to ensure your data is stored securely and protected from unauthorised access.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

How long we keep your information

We retain your repair records for 1 year from the date of your repair, in line with our warranty period. After this time your repair records will be deleted. If you have opted in to receive marketing communications, we will retain your contact details until you unsubscribe.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. By continuing to use our site you agree to our use of cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us on 07770 971253. We will respond and action your request within 30 days.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please call us on 07770 971253.
