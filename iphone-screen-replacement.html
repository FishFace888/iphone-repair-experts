Don't leave it too long

A cracked screen is more than just cosmetic

Most people think a cracked screen is just an inconvenience. In reality, a damaged screen gets worse over time and can cause real problems if left unrepaired.

👆
Touch sensitivity degrades
Cracks damage the digitiser layer beneath the glass. Over time this causes touch to become unresponsive in certain areas, making your phone harder to use every day. Scrolling, typing and tapping all become unreliable. This gets worse, not better.
⚫
Dead pixels appear and spread
Pressure from a crack can cause dark spots or dead pixels on the display. These spread over time as internal pressure increases. What starts as a small black patch in a corner can grow until the screen becomes unusable.
🎨
Colour and display problems
Cracks compromise the display layers beneath the glass. You may notice colour bleeding, washed out areas, pink or green lines, or sections of the screen with reduced brightness and contrast. These problems worsen over time.
💸
The longer you wait, the more it costs
A cracked screen that is cosmetic today can become a full display failure tomorrow. Replacing the screen now is significantly cheaper than waiting until dead pixels, touch failure or internal damage sets in and the repair becomes more complex.
💧
Moisture and dust get in
Your iPhone's water resistance relies on a sealed screen unit. A cracked screen breaks that seal. Dust, moisture and debris can enter through cracks and cause damage to the internal components, going well beyond just the display.
🔪
Sharp edges and risk of cuts
A cracked screen does not stay still. Glass continues to splinter further with every drop, flex or pocket entry. Over time the edges can become sharp enough to catch fingers, particularly around the corners and edges of the screen.
What we actually do

We replace the complete screen assembly

A common misunderstanding about screen repair is that the technician simply replaces the outer glass. Some cheaper repairers do exactly that, bonding new glass over the cracked original. It may look fixed on the surface, but it often performs poorly and fails quickly.

We replace the complete screen assembly. On modern iPhones this is a single bonded unit containing three functional elements that work together:

The outer glass is the protective surface you touch every day. The touch layer beneath it detects your input and translates it into precise on-screen response. The display panel behind that produces the image, with all the colour, brightness and contrast your iPhone was designed to deliver.

Because these are bonded together as one assembly, replacing the whole unit is the correct repair. It restores your screen to full function, not just appearance.

🔲
Outer glass
The protective surface you touch. Replaced as part of the complete assembly, not bonded over the original crack.
👆
Touch layer
Detects and translates every tap, swipe and scroll. A new assembly restores full touch accuracy across the entire screen.
🖥️
Display panel
Produces colour, brightness and contrast. We use high quality panels selected to deliver performance as close to the original as possible.
